Our Story!           Our coffees and teas come straight from the source. Many of our products are grown and made by indigenous tribes that have lived in their respective areas for centuries. The above picture is a painting from an indigenous tribe in the North American region. That same tribe's teas are sold on our website and can be delivered to you!
This is Andrew! He is the founder and CEO of Global Coffee. A former over-the-road truck driver, he had a vision when traveling accross the U.S. to share his experience with the rest of the world that he was having with native local tribes. He loved the coffees and teas he would drink at different tribal grounds and decided to make his own company and that was how Global Coffee was born!
We believe in giving back to the community and helping people who are in need. Most of the tribes who do business with our company live in poverty or near poverty levels. For this reason, we created a non-profit charitable organization called Global Care. A portion of the Proceeds from every purchase made on our website is donated to Global Care. You can rest assure that when you do business with us that you are helping the very people who work hard to bring the coffees and teas you absolutely love to your doorstep!
